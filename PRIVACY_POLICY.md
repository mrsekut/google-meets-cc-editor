# Privacy Policy for Google Meets CC Editor

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Overview
Google Meets CC Editor ("the Extension") is a browser extension that captures Google Meet captions and accumulates them in an editable floating panel.

## Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All caption text is processed locally within your browser tab and is never sent to any external server.

## Data Usage
No user data is collected or used.

## Data Storage
Caption text is stored temporarily in the browser tab's memory during a meeting session. No data is persisted to disk or sent externally.

## Data Sharing
This extension does not share any data with third parties.

## Permissions
- `host_permissions: https://meet.google.com/*` — Required to inject the content script into Google Meet pages in order to observe and capture live captions displayed on the page.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
